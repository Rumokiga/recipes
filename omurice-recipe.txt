Ingredients:
	- 1 onion
	- 4 slices bacon
	- 1 cup frozen peas
	- 1 cup frozen corn
	- 2 cups rice
	- 2 tablespoons ketchup
	- 2 eggs

Directions:
	1. Chop onion. Saute bacon and onion.
	2. When translucent add peas and corn. Cook till tender.
	3. Add cooked rice and ketchup and fry until combined.
	4. Make a thin omelet and spoon rice mixture inside. Fold over and add ketchup to your liking.
